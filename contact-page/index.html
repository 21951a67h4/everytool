Frequently Asked Questions

How can I request a new tool?

You can request a new tool by filling out our contact form. Select "Tool Request" as the subject and provide details about the tool you'd like us to add. We evaluate all requests and prioritize them based on user demand and feasibility.

Are all the tools free to use?

Yes, all tools currently available on Everytool.tech are completely free to use. We may introduce premium features in the future, but we're committed to keeping core functionality free for everyone.

How do I report a bug?

If you encounter any issues while using our tools, please use the contact form and select "Report a Bug" as the subject. Be sure to provide detailed information about the problem, including the tool name, steps to reproduce the issue, and your browser information.

Can I use your tools for commercial purposes?

Yes, our tools can be used for both personal and commercial purposes. However, we ask that you don't redistribute our tools as your own or embed them in paid products without permission. If you have specific commercial use case questions, please contact us.

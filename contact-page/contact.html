Frequently Asked Questions

Are all the tools really free to use?

Yes, all tools on Everytool.tech are completely free to use. We generate revenue through non-intrusive advertisements to keep the service free for everyone.

How do I request a new tool?

You can request a new tool by filling out the contact form above. Select "Tool Request" from the subject dropdown and provide details about the tool you'd like to see added.

How do you handle my data?

We take your privacy seriously. Most of our tools process data directly in your browser without sending it to our servers. For contact form submissions, we only use your information to respond to your inquiry. Please see our Privacy Policy for more details.

Can I use your tools for commercial purposes?

Yes, you can use our tools for personal or commercial purposes. However, automated access or embedding our tools directly into other applications is not permitted without prior written consent.

How often do you add new tools?

We aim to add new tools every week based on user requests and our development capacity. We prioritize tools that are most requested by our users.

How to Use

Adding Time

Enter hours, minutes, and seconds in the input fields and click "Calculate Total Time". You can add more than two time values by clicking "Add Another Time".

Subtracting Time

Enter the larger time value in the first row and the time to subtract in the second row. Click "Calculate Time Difference" to get the result.

Converting Time

Enter a value, select the original time unit and the unit you want to convert to, then click "Convert".

Duration Calculator

Select start and end dates and times, then click "Calculate Duration" to find the exact time span between them.

Time Units

Time calculations use the following standard conversions:

  • 1 minute = 60 seconds
  • 1 hour = 60 minutes
  • 1 day = 24 hours
  • 1 week = 7 days
  • 1 month = 30 days (for simplicity)
  • 1 year = 365 days (standard year)

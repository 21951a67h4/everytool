How to Use the Age Calculator

This age calculator helps you determine your exact age in years, months, days, and even hours and minutes.

To calculate your age:

  1. Enter your date of birth using either the date picker or by manually entering the day, month, and year.
  2. By default, your age will be calculated as of today's date.
  3. If you want to calculate your age as of a different date, check the "Calculate age as of a specific date" option and enter the reference date.
  4. Click the "Calculate Age" button to see your results.

Age Calculation Method

This calculator uses precise date arithmetic to calculate your exact age. It accounts for leap years, variable month lengths, and other calendar complexities to give you accurate results.

Did You Know?

Different cultures have different ways of calculating age. For example, in some East Asian countries, you're considered 1 year old at birth, and everyone adds a year to their age on New Year's Day, rather than on their birthday.
